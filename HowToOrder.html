How to Order?

1) Send an email to superfreshbags@superfreshbags.com.au with details of your order, including your name, mailing address, and how you intend to pay.
Or
2) Call us on 02-9631 0143 and provide the above information.

Payment
These are the available options:-

A) Direct Credit or EFT to Super Fresh Bags Bank Account.
Bank Name: St George Bank
A/c Name: Super Fresh Bags
BSB No: 114 - 879
A/c No: 4310 80559
Note: Please ensure that your name is printed in the Reference.

B) PayPal / Visa / MasterCard
On your browser type "PayPal". The PayPal Home Page should appear.
You can also access the PayPal website from Super Fresh Bags website. Click on the link https://www.paypal.com
Sign up for Free. Follow the prompts - you will need your email address, a secret password and your credit card details to sign-up.
Once set up, choose make a Payment. You will have a choice of making a PayPal, Visa or MasterCard payment.
Once prompted, type in "superfreshbags@superfreshbags.com.au" - this is the account you are paying into.

C) Cheque
Cheque made out to Super Fresh Bags. Mail the cheque to:-

Note: Please ensure that details of the order, your full name and mailing address is provided.
