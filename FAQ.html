How do Super Fresh Bags work?

All vegetables and fruit give off ethylene gas as they deteriorate over time. Warm temperature and exposure to light speeds the deterioration. Hence the banana left on the kitchen counter ripens faster than when kept in a cool part of the home.

Super Fresh Bags are specially designed, multi-layered and laminated food bags manufactured using food grade storage materials. These films or layers provide barriers to light and oxygen, both of which are necessary to hasten ripening of fruit and ageing of vegetables. Super Fresh Bags provide this essential protection and keep fruit and vegetables fresh and lasting longer.

This is why Super Fresh Bags do not have a clear window to identify the contents. However, the contents can be identified easily by writing on the white bag.

How to use Super Fresh Bags?

Super Fresh Bags have dual purpose. They keep vegetables and fruit fresh between 2 to 4 weeks in the refrigerator. The bags also keep fresh dry, processed foods such as potato chips, crackers, biscuits, nuts, lollies and pet treats, in the pantry or refrigerator. The bags come with seals BUT DO NOT SEAL for fresh vegetables and fruit. However, when storing dry and processed foods, expel as much air as possible and SEAL the bags. The seals should be opened carefully as they are NOT designed for constant use.

Are Super Fresh Bags proven?

Freshness is one of the main attributes consumers look for when choosing fruits and vegetables. In spite of its importance, the understanding and perception of freshness by the consumer vary widely as it is determined by sensory and physio-chemical characteristics related to freshness. Hence, taste, aroma, crispness, juiciness, colour, textural properties, and physiological aging are descriptors used to assess freshness. Sensory perceptions are very personal as well as gender-based. In other words, it is very subjective.

Simply put, there is no laboratory-based methodology to scientifically test freshness in vegetables and fruits using the above descriptors. So, Super Fresh Bags were tested in the best laboratory in the world – in the kitchen at home! For over four years, Super Fresh Bags were tested on a wide variety of fresh vegetables and fruits with consistent and excellent results demonstrating freshness as described by taste, crispness, juiciness, aroma, colour, textual properties, and physiological aging (deterioration).

Yes, analyses were subjective but the assessors were consistent. So will you be, when you evaluate freshness with Super Fresh Bags.

The choice of food-grade materials for the design of the bag was also evaluated extensively using samples provided by nine manufacturers of food packaging.

These are reusable bags that can withstand constant use for extended periods, provided they are rinsed and dried after each use. Customers have used the same bags for over two years. They should be replaced if they become grubby.

Why are there three sizes of Super Fresh Bags?

We offer three sizes with storage capacities (by weight) of 500 grams, 1 kg and 2 kg. The average household will purchase a variety of fresh vegetables and fruits and consequently, a variety of bags will be required. For lettuce, celery and other leafy vegetables, a 2kg bag will be required. Some bulky vegetables such as broccoli and cauliflower will also require the 2kg bags. Cut cabbage, cut pumpkin, cut cauliflower are easily stored in the 2 kg bags. Carrots, cucumbers, corn can be packed in 1 kg bags. The 500 gram bag is ideal for grapes, strawberries, peaches, etc and avocado, capsicum, etc.

Should Super Fresh Bags be sealed?

A re-sealable strip is provided. However, when storing fresh vegetables and fruits do not seal the bag. The seal is provided for storing items that require an air-tight environment such as dried and processed fruits and nuts, chips, cereals, pet-treats, etc.

How to identify the contents inside Super Fresh Bags

Before the bags are put away in the refrigerator, write the contents on the white panels of the bag. Writing on the base is also an option.

Alternatively, stickers or labels can be placed in a visible spot on the bag. Colour-coded stickers can distinguish fruits from vegetables. Stickers with fruits and vegetable icons are another option.

By placing vegetables and fruits in specific parts of the fridge, it is possible to identify those items easily.

Can Super Fresh Bags be used to store fresh fruit and left on the kitchen table?

These bags are meant for use in the refrigerator in order to keep fresh fruit and vegetables longer. However, we suggest experimenting. For example, one customer advised that tomatoes have been kept on the kitchen counter in the bags and remained fresh.

How many Super Fresh Bags should a household have?

Use a separate bag for each variety of fresh vegetables or fresh fruit.

Typically, a large household which has high weekly fresh vegetables and fruits purchase will require as many bags as possible to store them. Depending on the volume of vegetables and fruits to be stored, the full range of bags will be required.

In addition to this, it is advisable to have spare bags so that a fresh set of bags are always available for the following week’s fresh vegetable and fruit storage.
