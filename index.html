Super Fresh Bags must be sealed when storing processed and dried fruits and nuts. These bags lock in flavours and nutritional goodness (vitamins and minerals). The re-closeable seal guards against spoilage. Expel all air before sealing.

Other applications

Super Fresh Bags make the ideal protein powder packaging. The laminated multiple layers of scientifically designed film protect and extend the life of the contents by keeping out moisture, vapour, odour, and pests while locking in freshness and nutritional vibrancy.

When storing dry products the bag must be sealed. As the bags have a sturdy base and stable sides they can be stored easily in the pantry

Rice, pet treats, consumables (sugar, salt, etc) and health foods can be stored in Super Fresh Bags. Seal as appropriate. Refrigerate as required.

Super Fresh Bags can be used as lunch bags.

Other crazy and weird uses

Super Fresh Bags can be reused/recycled and are suitable for many other applications such as storage bags, flower pots, storing plant seeds, garden fertilizer pellets, etc.

Take note of weight restrictions on bag capacity. They can also be used as a “cooler bag” or ice bag” for chilling wine, beer and soft drinks, etc. Ensure that the base is supported. Instead of throwing away the old bags use them as garbage bags.
